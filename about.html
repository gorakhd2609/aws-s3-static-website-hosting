About Us

Welcome to ComfortHome, where tradition meets modernity. Rooted in Indian heritage, we craft furniture and decor inspired by Maharashtrian jharokhas, Mughal inlays, and South Indian teak woodwork. Our artisans blend cultural richness with contemporary design to bring you timeless pieces.

Founded in 2024, we aim to celebrate India's craftsmanship while offering comfort to every home.
